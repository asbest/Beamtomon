# Beamtomon
# Idea
You play a Beamter in the world of Beamtentum. You go through a world of offices and you meet consultants, politicians, Citizens, other Beamte. Most of them are hostile and you must fight against them. You must fight against opponents to level up and to reach several levels of promotions.

# Principle
World and play mechanics shall look like in the Pokémon games. Fights are like in Pokémon: Turn based attack selection. Attacks are funny Beamten related like permission denied or not responsible. Consultants can be catched and then they fight for the player.
